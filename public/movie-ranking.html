Best Movies According to Deeksha

My top 3 Movies of all-time

The Shawshank Redemption

Released in 1994, this movie is a masterpiece of storytelling and character development. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongfully imprisoned

The Godfather

Released in 1972, this movie is a classic crime drama that explores the world of organized crime. It follows the story of the Corleone family, led by patriarch Vito Corleone.

The Dark Knight

Released in 2008, this movie is a superhero film that redefined the genre. It features the iconic performance of Heath Ledger as the Joker, and explores themes of chaos and morality.
